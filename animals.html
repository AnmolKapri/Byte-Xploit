Buffaloes

Diseases: Foot-and-Mouth Disease (FMD)

Goat

Common Diseases: Foot-and-Mouth Disease (FMD)

Solutions: Implementing good biosecurity measures, such as restricting visitor access, disinfecting equipment, and isolating new animals, can significantly reduce the risk of disease transmission.

Plant:Chickens

Diseases:Bird Flu

Description: This is a significant concern due to its potential to spread rapidly and its zoonotic nature. Outbreaks often lead to the culling of entire flocks to prevent further spread. Symptoms include respiratory distress, a drop in egg production, and sudden death.

Solutions: Promptly identifying and reporting sick animals to veterinary authorities is essential for controlling outbreaks before they can spread.
